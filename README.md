## Overview
This repository serves as a comprehensive portfolio for the coursework and projects completed during the AI Applications (ITAI-2372) semester. It documents my exploration into the technical, ethical, and societal implications of artificial intelligence across various industries, from healthcare and manufacturing to smart city management and retail.

---

## Repository Structure

### 1. 📂 Lecture Notes
The `Lecture Notes` folder contains detailed technical analysis and key takeaways from the core modules of the course:

* **Module 02: Recent Advancements & Future Trends**
    * Study of Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) vs. Narrow AI.
    * Exploration of AI in space exploration, including autonomous robotics for spacecraft assembly.
    * Core technical proficiencies in Python and deep learning libraries like TensorFlow.
* **Module 03 & 04: AI in Healthcare & Ethics**
    * Analysis of robotic-assisted surgery systems such as the da Vinci and STAR (Smart Tissue Autonomous Robot).
    * Overview of AI-driven healthcare management for administrative efficiency.
    * In-depth study of algorithmic bias in medical datasets leading to treatment inequities.
* **Module 04 (Part 2): AI in Agriculture**
    * Research into precision farming and the risks of autonomous drone pesticide drift.
    * Analysis of data privacy in yield data and the preservation of traditional farming knowledge.
* **Module 05: Smart Cities**
    * Examination of the "Urban Brain" using IoT sensors, 5G, and edge computing.
    * Technological focus on V2X (Vehicle-to-Everything) communication for optimized traffic.
* **Module 06: Media & Entertainment**
    * Study of generative art using Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) and AI-driven music composition.
    * Analysis of personalized recommendation systems (Netflix, Spotify) and the threat of deepfakes.
* **Module 07: Blockchain Integration**
    * Research on using decentralized ledgers for data integrity and AI audit trails.
* **Module 08: AI in Retail**
    * Exploration of omnichannel personalization, cashierless checkout (Computer Vision), and demand forecasting.

### 2. 📂 Case Studies
The `Case Studies` folder hosts in-depth research papers on industry-specific AI applications and original proposals:

* **A09: Autonomous Vehicles (AVs) & Ethics**
    * Analysis of SAE automation levels and the "Trolley Problem" in path-planning logic.
    * Research on demographic bias in pedestrian detection models.
* **A10: Public Sector - Traffic Orchestration**
    * Evaluation of AI-driven Emergency Vehicle Preemption (EVP) systems like "Glance".
    * **Original Proposal:** "The Digital Moses" Protocol—using DRL and V2X to clear traffic corridors for responders before sirens are audible.
* **A11: Manufacturing - Predictive Maintenance**
    * Case study on General Motors' (GM) Zero Downtime (ZDT) system for Fanuc robots.
    * **Original Proposal:** Acoustic-Neural Quality Guard (ANQG)—a system that "listens" to weld signatures to detect internal micro-cracks.
* **A12: Municipal Management - The Civic Mind**
    * Proposal for leveraging Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) to manage municipal park resources objectively.
    * Integration of Stoic logic to ensure equitable resource distribution.
    * **Tool Highlight:** Development of the "Park Pulse" on-site reporting tool for real-time infrastructure impact assessment.
* **A04: Financial Services - Fraud Detection**
    * Research on Danske Bank’s deep learning platform, resulting in a 60% reduction in false positives.
